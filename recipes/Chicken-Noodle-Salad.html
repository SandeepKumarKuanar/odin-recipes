Chicken Noodle Salad

A few needed details to consider

  • Preparation time: 20 mins or less
  • Cooking time: 15-20 mins or less
  • Serving per Preparation: 4
  • Calories: 441g
  • Protein: 20g
  • Fat: 12g(this are essential fats)
  • Carbs: 65g

Final Product

List of required ingredients

  • 3 cloves fresh garlic, prefer crushed
  • 1/2 cup of pre-seasoned rice vinegar
  • 1 tablespoon soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon fish sauce
  • 1 tablespoon brown sugar
  • 1 tablespoon Sriracha chile sauce
  • 1 tablespoon hoisin sauce
  • 1 teaspoon sesame oil
  • 1 properly cooked chicken breast
  • 1 package spaghetti
  • 1 cup grated carrots
  • 1 red bell pepper, thinly sliced
  • 4 green onions, thinly sliced
  • ½ cup chopped roasted, salted peanuts
  • ⅓ cup chopped fresh basil
  • ⅓ cup chopped fresh mint
  • ⅓ cup chopped cilantro

Steps to be followed(need for more skill to bring together)

  1. Whisk garlic, rice vinegar, soy sauce, fish sauce, brown sugar, Sriracha, hoisin, and sesame oil together in a mixing bowl for the dressing.
  2. Tear chicken apart into strands nearly similar(eyeball them) in width to the spaghetti. Refrigerate until ready to use.
  3. Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Cook spaghetti in the boiling water, stirring occasionally, until tender yet firm to the bite, about 12 minutes. Drain and rinse with cold water.
  4. Transfer spaghetti to a mixing bowl; add the chicken, carrots, bell pepper, and green onions.
  5. Pour in the dressing and toss by hand until well combined.
  6. Seal the surface of the salad with plastic wrap and refrigerate, tossing occasionally, for 2 to 12 hours, the longer the better.
  7. Add peanuts, basil, mint, and cilantro to the salad. Toss well; taste and adjust for seasoning and salt.
