Final Product

Tiny Description

This gorgeous limoncello sunrise cocktail mimics the stunning colors(not artificial)often found in a sunrise.

Adding an orange slice and a maraschino cherry make it extra nice for a special occasion.

List of required ingredients

  • 2 fluid ounces limoncello
  • 4 fluid ounces orange juice
  • 1 splash grenadine
  • ice cubes (optional)
  • 1 maraschino cherry (optional)
  • 1 orange slice (optional)

Steps to be followed(simple to bring together)

  1. Add ice cubes to an Old Fashioned glass. Pour in limoncello and orange juice.
  2. Slowly pour in grenadine from a squeeze bottle (It will sink to the bottom of the glass).
  3. Garnish with an orange slice and a maraschino cherry. Serve immediately.
  4. If you chill the ingredients, you will not need ice cubes.
